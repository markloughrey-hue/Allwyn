A bespoke CVM model built for you — that we never see the output of

Enrichment tells you what a customer can afford. A CVM model tells you what they're actually worth. We build a bespoke model for your team to a shared specification. You run your behavioural data through it on your side; CRED supplies the external layer that completes it. The score that ranks your individuals stays entirely inside Allwyn.

The model runs as a shared specification — weighted parameters that take all inputs and output a single 1–5 value tier (5 = top customer). Because it runs inside your environment, CRED never sees the ranked individuals. We provide the engine and the external layer; you keep the answer.

Why two axes beat one number

The tier isn't just a score — it's an action

Plotting what a customer can afford against what they're actually worth to you produces a map no operator can build alone, because the affordability axis is missing today. Each quadrant tells marketing, CRM and compliance exactly what to do.
